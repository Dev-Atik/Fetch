Meet Fetch.

There was something missing when it came to caring for my puppy in an eco-friendly way, leading me to create Fetch — a vegan leather pet accessories for the conscientious consumers.

Inspired by our Grandma

Our Swedish grandmother instilled in us the values of community, animal care, and environmental consciousness, inspiring us to bring sustainable pet products to the market when we realized the pet industry lacked such solutions.

Our sustainable practices

Our strong webbing are made from recycled PET bottles. Billions of plastic bottles go into landfills every year. But now you and your dog can do something about it!

Our eco-sustainable leather is made from corn. Our leather is 100% cruelty free and PETA certified. Made from corn crop residue, mixed with recycled polyester for durability.

Giving back

Our pet products prioritize sustainability and animal welfare, and make it easy for pet owners to make ethical purchases that reduce their environmental footprint while also supporting the SPCA foundation.

Our mission is to make the world of dog -owning more sustainable

Johan - Founder of Fetch
